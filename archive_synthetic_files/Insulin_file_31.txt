*   **Becoming a Lifelong Learner and Skill Developer:** Insulin believes that growth is a continuous process. She is driven by a curiosity to understand the world and herself more deeply. Her aspirations include:
    *   **Expanding her knowledge base:** Whether it's delving into historical periods, exploring scientific advancements, or understanding different cultures, she aims to continuously broaden her intellectual horizons.
